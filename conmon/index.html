Solutions to Convex Analysis and Monotone Operator Theory book

I recently started with some exercises of the comprehensive convex analysis and monotone operator book by Bauschke and Combettes. It is an excellent source for self-studying and a good fit for a student with EE math background.

I publish some of the solutions here to self-motivate myself to do more 😁 You can find the entry notes at
